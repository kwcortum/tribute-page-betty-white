Betty White

America's grandmother, the last of the Golden Girls

A timeline of Betty White's life:

  • January 17th, 1922: Born in Oak Park, Illinois.
  • March 15th, 1939: Ms. White started modeling before acting.
  • 1941-1951: Had many roles on good radio shows.
  • 1946: Lands her role on television, i.e. The Daily Show.
  • June 14th, 1963: Ms. White marries Mr. Allen Ludden.
  • 1975 and 1976: Ms. White wins emmy for outstanding continuing performance for the The Mary Tyler Moore Show.
  • 1985-1992: Ms. White assumes the role of Rose Nyland for her hit show "The Golden Girls."
  • 1986: Ms. White wins emmy for the "The Golden Girls."
  • 1992-2021: Ms. White guests stars on many shows including Saturday Night Live.
  • December 31st, 2021: Ms. White passes away.
